Custom Builds

Your bike should be as unique as you are. That's why Vicious Cycles offers custom build electric bicycles, made right in our shop with the highest‑quality parts tailored specific to your needs.

Step 1

Meet with our builders to discuss your build. We'll work with you to make your dream bike a reality. Set up an appointment through our contact form or come by the shop and we'll schedule your meeting.

Step 2

Our builders will create a mock‑up of how your bike will look and what features it will have. We want to prioritize your needs, so you'll meet again to review the mock‑up and make any final improvements.

Step 3

Our builders will begin construction on your custom cycle. This process will take 6‑8 weeks depending on part order times and build complexity. Construction of your bike will not begin until you've made a 25% down payment.

Step 4

You'll have a final meeting with the builders to test ride your custom cycle and discuss any final tweaks. Any changes may require additional build time, no more than 3 weeks. If your bike is perfect and we've received full payment, you're all set. Enjoy the freedom of a new custom build cycle!
