Vicious Cycles

Vicious Cycles, Inc. is a Raleigh-based cycle workshop founded by a few like-minded individuals in late 2010. We hope to create and foster a strong passion for the local biking scene because each and every one of us is a biking enthusiast. We believe that every machine should be an extension of its owner. We believe in the significance of individualism and freedom.

We build custom electric bicycles primarily for commuters, because we know there are no greater freedoms than no traffic jams, no road rage, no searching for parking, and no gas!

The Mechanic

Jane began working on bicycles in college, when the chain broke on her bike. With no bike shops around and no method of transportation, she taught herself how to repair her bike and quickly learned how to fix any and all problems that could come up. She specializes in electric bike repair, but loves repairing all kinds of bikes!

The Builder

Josh builds all of our custom cycles. His love for bikes began as a child, riding around town with his family and learning to fix any mechanical issues himself. As he grew up, he began modifying his cycles to fit his needs and building his own bicycle. For three years he's spreading his love of custom cycles to our customers!
