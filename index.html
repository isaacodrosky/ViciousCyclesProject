Custom Builds

Your bike should be as unique as you, that's why we'll work with you to create a custom bike that perfectly meets your needs! From stylish cycles to trusty monster rides, we've done them all. Get in touch with a member of our mechanic team to discuss your plan, and we'll see your dream realized.

Full-Service Repairs

Our team of passionate mechanics have over 50 combined years of experience repairing bikes. We have a guaranteed 24-hour turnaround for most repairs. If we can't have it done within 24 hours, we'll loan you a bike until yours is repaired. Please contact us to schedule an appointment for repair.

Financing Options

We know quality bikes aren't cheap, that's why we have in-house loan options to help finance your purchase. All custom builds require a 25% down payment before projects begin, and either payment in full, an approved in-house loan, or a transfer of funds to a third-party vendor before delivery.
